
ZENDESK (Software to clone)
Zendesk is a cloud-based customer service platform that helps businesses improve their communication with customers. It offers a variety of tools, including: 
    Helpdesk: Tracks, prioritizes, and solves customer tickets
    Help center: A knowledge base that organizes frequently asked questions and product features
    Chat: Provides quick help and creates a personal connection with customers
    Email marketing: Helps businesses market to their customers
    Sales: Helps businesses sell their products
    Employee engagement: Helps businesses engage with their employees
Zendesk also uses AI to help customers and employees. This includes: 
    AI agents: Autonomous bots that can work alone or with human agents
    Workforce management: Optimizes team productivity and quality assurance
    Quality assurance: Transforms your business
